Our Story

Why we started CherriCode

There are a lot of Shopify developers out there. Freelancers who’ll build you something in a week. Big agencies that charge five times what the job is worth and assign a junior dev after the sales call.

Neither option is great for a founder who needs a store that performs — and someone they can actually reach when something breaks six months later.

That’s the gap CherriCode fills. We’re a small, focused Shopify agency. We don’t try to do everything. We do Shopify well — across fashion, wellness, food, home goods, electronics, and more.

We’ve handled first launches for founders who had never sold online, and we’ve optimized stores doing multiple six figures that were leaving money on the table with a checkout flow nobody had ever fixed.

We’re building CherriCode to be a long-term partner, not a one-off vendor. Down the road, we’ll expand into broader product development. But right now, Shopify is where we can be most useful.

“We don’t disappear after launch. That’s the one thing clients hear most from us — and what they miss most from other agencies.”

How We Operate

The values that shape every project

01
Honesty over comfort

If your idea won’t work, we tell you. If your timeline is unrealistic, we say so. We’d rather have a hard conversation early than a failed project later.

02
Outcomes over deliverables

We don’t measure success by what we shipped. We measure it by whether your store performs better. A beautiful store that doesn’t sell is a failed project.

03
Simple communication

No jargon. No disappearing for weeks. You’ll always know what we’re working on and what’s next. Short, clear updates on a regular cadence.

04
Priced to be fair

Fixed project prices. No hourly billing that spirals. No surprise invoices. We scope carefully and charge exactly what we said we would.

05
Long-term thinking

We make decisions that are good for your store in 12 months, not just at launch. That means recommending simpler, maintainable solutions when they’re right.

06
Accountable by default

You’ll know who is working on your store. Not a rotating team of subcontractors. Real people who are invested in your store performing.
